About Agape Family Church

Demonstrating God's unconditional love to all mankind — welcoming every person into relationship with Jesus Christ. ❤️

Visit Us Our Ministries

Our Mission

To demonstrate God's unconditional love (Agape) by making disciples, strengthening families, and serving our community. We are committed to clear biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, and practical compassion.

Our Vision

To be a growing family of believers who transform our neighbourhood through prayer, gospel-centered teaching, practical service and the empowerment of young leaders.

Core Values

  • Christ-centered — Scripture shapes everything we do.
  • Love — Unconditional compassion for all people.
  • Community — Life together, supporting one another.
  • Discipleship — Growing in faith, serving with skill.
  • Youth & Leadership — Developing the next generation of leaders.

What We Believe

We hold to historic Christian truth summarized briefly below. For a full statement of faith, please contact the church office.

The Bible

The Bible is God's inspired Word and is authoritative for faith and life.

God

There is one triune God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—creator and sustainer of all things.

Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ, fully God and fully man, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose again, and will return in glory.

Salvation

Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

Our Story

Agape Family Church began as a small group of believers who wanted to demonstrate God's love practically. Over time we have grown into a multi-generational congregation focused on discipleship, worship and community service.

  1. Founding: Small home group launched regular gatherings.
  2. First Building: Secured a meeting space and formalized ministries.
  3. Growth & Outreach: Expanded youth programs and community initiatives.
