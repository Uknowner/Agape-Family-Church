Our Mission & Vision

At Grace Community Church, we strive to be a place where people can encounter God, grow in faith, and serve our community with love and compassion.

Our vision is to see lives transformed by the power of the Gospel and to be a beacon of hope in our city.

Love God

Worshiping God with all our heart, soul, and mind

Serve Others

Showing Christ's love through practical service

Share Hope

Spreading the message of salvation to our community
