Our Mission

To demonstrate God's unconditional love (Agape) by leading people to Christ, strengthening families, and serving the community with compassion and truth.

Our Vision

To build a Christ-centered family church that transforms lives through the power of the Holy Spirit, reaching Katlehong and beyond with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Core Values

  • Faith: Trusting God in all things.
  • Love: Showing unconditional love to all.
  • Service: Serving God by serving others.
  • Unity: Building a strong family in Christ.
  • Growth: Equipping believers for purpose and leadership.
